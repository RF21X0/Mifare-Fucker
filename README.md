## üí° Project Overview

**Mifare Fucker App** is a native **Android** application developed for the **reading** and **decoding** of data contained within **MIFARE Classic 1K** chips.

By leveraging your device's NFC (Near Field Communication) capabilities, the app allows you to acquire and view in a structured format:

* The Unique Identifier (UID).
* The hexadecimal content of all **16 sectors** and **64 blocks** of the MIFARE 1K chip.

This tool is perfect for security professionals, hobbyists, and anyone who needs to analyze or diagnose the functionality of their own MIFARE tags.

## üöÄ Getting Started

Since the app is not available on the Google Play Store, you must download the APK directly.

1.  **Requirements**: Ensure your Android device has **NFC** functionality enabled.
2.  **Download**: Get the latest `MifareFucker.apk` file from the **[official website](https://mifare-fucker.matrix.wf/)**.
3.  **Installation**: You might need to enable "Install unknown apps" in your phone's security settings.
4.  **Usage**: Launch the app and follow the prompts to place your MIFARE 1K tag near the NFC antenna.
